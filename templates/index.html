Gee Tee General Engineering (Pty) Ltd was formed in 1980 with the sole purpose of providing the engineering industry with quality products and an efficient service. We have full in-house manufacturing facilities including CNC milling, CNC turning, EDM wire cutting, surface grinding, 3D printing, and laser engraving.

We offer cutting-edge design solutions for the rapid development of working prototypes. Our experience in the design and manufacture of many components for the automotive, mining, steel and food industries is extensive. Once a working design has been developed, we produce working prototypes for testing and further design refinement. Our commitment to the client continues throughout the process until a successful design has been achieved.

Gee Tee Engineering's pursuit of excellence ensures our staff are equipped with up-to-date training while we continue to improve our machinery in line with current technological advancements. Our quality products and commitment to client satisfaction have earned the company a loyal and growing multinational client base.

We achieve standard tolerances of ±0.01mm on CNC turning and milling, ±0.005mm on surface grinding, and ±0.005mm with Ra 0.2μm surface finish on EDM wire cutting. Tighter tolerances are available on request depending on material and geometry. Our CNC machines are regularly calibrated to maintain these standards.

We machine a wide range of materials: carbon steels (mild steel, EN8, EN19, EN24), stainless steels (304, 316, 316L, duplex), tool steels (D2, H13, M2, S7), aluminium alloys (6061, 7075, 2024), brass and copper, and engineering plastics (Nylon, Delrin, PEEK, UHMWPE). Our EDM wire cutter handles hardened materials up to 62 HRC.

Our manufacturing processes feature stringent quality control. Every component passes through rigorous inspection using calibrated measuring instruments. We provide full material traceability and certificates of conformance on request, ensuring your parts meet the highest engineering standards.

Our largest CNC milling envelope is 1400 × 700 × 600mm on the Doosan DNM6700 machining centre. The Agie Charmilles EDM wire cutter handles 600 × 400 × 350mm. Our CNC lathes offer a maximum turning capacity of 700mm in diameter and 2000mm in length for shafts, sleeves, and rotational components.

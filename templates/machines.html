CNC Turning Services — Precision Lathework in Germiston

Our CNC turning capability covers a wide range of rotational components including shafts, bushings, flanges, couplings, bearing housings and custom fittings. We operate multiple horizontal CNC lathes with swings up to 700mm and between-centres distances up to 2000mm.

We feature live tooling with Y-axis capabilities, enabling milling, drilling and tapping operations to be completed in a single setup — eliminating secondary operations and reducing lead time.

EDM Wire Cutting — Complex Profiles & Hard Materials

Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) wire cutting allows us to produce intricate, tight-tolerance profiles in materials that are too hard to machine conventionally. Our Agie Charmilles Cut E 600 and Fanuc Robocut aC400iC are among the world's leading EDM machines, capable of cutting through hardened tool steel, carbide, and exotic alloys with micron-level precision.

This service is essential for die and punch tooling, complex extrusion profiles, gear teeth, keyways and any geometry where traditional cutting tools cannot reach.

Hardened Steel Die & Punch Tooling Complex Profiles Tight Tolerances Exotic Alloys

Key Equipment

  • Agie Charmilles Cut E 600 — 600×400×350mm
  • Fanuc Robocut aC400iC — 400×300×250mm
  • Micron-level precision
  • Hard material capability

Reverse Engineering — Obsolete Part Replication

When original drawings are lost, suppliers no longer exist, or parts are discontinued, our reverse engineering service gives manufacturers a lifeline. Our in-house designers can precisely recreate any component in strict accordance with the physical sample, reproducing it with our full suite of CNC equipment.

This service is particularly valuable for mining operations during plant shutdowns, vintage automotive restoration, and replacing OEM parts from discontinued production lines.

Obsolete Parts Mining Spares Legacy Components OEM Replacement CAD Recreation

Process

  • CAD recreation (Edgecam/SolidWorks)
  • Material specification
  • CNC machining to drawing
  • Quality verification
